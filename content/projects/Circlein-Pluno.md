Pluno (Circlein) is a 3D workspace for design and collaboration; I worked across frontend and backend features. I built the landing page and dashboard, collaborating with a three.js engineer to integrate 3D experiences. I implemented the frontend with Next.js and optimized performance and resource rendering. I integrated Web3 (web3.js) and WalletConnect to enable user wallet connections and NFT workflows. I also wrote JavaScript utilities for uploading and rendering 2D/3D assets used by the product.
